01

Stunning Eleganza

While BLACKPINK'S commitment to fashion has never wavered, the group's aesthetic has changed and matured throughout their historic, record-breaking career. When they first burst onto the scene with hits like "Whistle" and "Boombayah," the girls favored bold, punchy streetwear with bright pops of color and cute schoolgirl-leaning ensembles. (Just look, for example, at Jisoo and Lisa pleated skirts or Rosé's fishnet tee emblazoned with “Beyond the Kawaii” in the "Boombayah" music video.)

02

Stunning Creativity

Image result for stunning dance moves of blackpink members The group's main dancer, Lisa, has precise, enthralling, and captivating dance abilities. Jennie, on the other hand, has sassy moves and adds her own flair during the choreography. Jisoo puts on a cheerful vibe during the group's performance with her always dazzling smile and crisp moves.

03

Loved by Millions

When it comes to other members of Blackpink, they all have a huge fan following as well. Jennie has 68.2 million people on her Insta, Jisoo has 62.1 million, and Rosé has 61 million. While talking about the band, Lisa and the rest of the members recently hit the headlines after ranking at the top of girl group brand reputation rankings.

04

Nice Personality

Jennie always steps up to embrace a leadership role on behalf of the group whenever needed. Advocates like Jisoo are rare personality types who make it their mission to do good and make the world a better place. Fierce rapper and dancer Lisa never forgets to channel her roots. Adventurers like Rosé are charming and agile. They are true artists who see life as a canvas for self-expression. Their uniqueness is driven by curiosity and their love to experiment and try new things.
